Why Cape Town?

Cape Town, the mother city

Cape Town offers a captivating blend of natural beauty, vibrant culture, and rich history, making it a must-visit destination for tourists. With its iconic Table Mountain, stunning beaches, and breathtaking coastal drives like Chapman’s Peak, the city is a paradise for nature lovers. Visitors can explore the historic V&A Waterfront, savor diverse cuisine, and experience the unique mix of cultures. Cape Town's wine estates and wildlife reserves add to its unmatched charm and allure.

Cape Town is a destination that caters to every traveler’s interests. Adventure seekers can hike Table Mountain, surf at Muizenberg Beach, or go cage diving with great white sharks. The city’s cultural landmarks, like Robben Island and the District Six Museum, tell stories of resilience and transformation. Meanwhile, the Cape Winelands offer wine-tasting experiences amid scenic vineyards, and the vibrant nightlife reflects Cape Town's energetic, welcoming spirit.

My favorite restaurants in Cape Town

The Bungalow Restaurant Clifton

Perfectly situated in the heart of Clifton between the Twelve Apostles, Lion’s Head and the Atlantic Ocean, it’s the best place in Cape Town to experience golden hour with a sundowner in hand and live DJs spinning your sunset soundtrack. The only restaurant located along the world-renowned Clifton reserve, it’s a standalone icon.

Address:

The Glen Country Club, No. 3 Victoria Road, Clifton

What I like about it

This is one of the best restaurants in Cape Town but if you go at any time of day there will be something delicious on the menu!

Bilboa Camps Bay

Sophisticated dining with the flavours of the Med, Camps Bay style, overlooking the Atlantic Ocean and Lion’s Head.

Address:

Shop 120, First Floor, The Promenade

What I like about it

Amazing food and great views. Came here many times during my trip in Cape Town and can’t fault it. From steaks to seafood – they have a everything you can think for a great lunch or dinner.

Grand Africa Café Beach

Grand Africa Café & Beach embraces a free-spirited beach café culture, nestled poignantly on the sea-front shoreline and flaunting her majestic views of the mighty Atlantic and Robben Island.

Address:

1 Haul Road, V&A Waterfront

What I like about it

With a choice of five bars, ocean-view decks, nine private function venue areas, and on-beach dining, you’re sure to find the perfect spot to capture that unforgettable magic, that is simply Grand!
